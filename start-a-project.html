Start a Project

Have an idea, a broken part, or a mechanical problem that needs a better solution? Start here.

How to Reach Out

The best way to begin is with a short description of what you are trying to build, improve, replace, or prototype. Photos, rough dimensions, sketches, and examples are all helpful.

Helpful Information to Include

Project goal
What are you trying to solve, build, or improve?

Existing part or object
Is there a broken, missing, or discontinued component involved?

Photos or measurements
Images, dimensions, and rough sketches make the process much easier.

Use case
How will the part or prototype actually be used?

Timeline
Is this exploratory, or do you need something by a certain date?

Typical Project Types

Prairie Printworks is best suited for prototype ideas, replacement parts, reverse engineering, workshop fixtures, accessibility-related solutions, and one-off mechanical problem solving.
